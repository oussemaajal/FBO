You are participating in a study on ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) evaluation.

Background
ESG rating agencies evaluate companies on three pillars—Environmental (E), Social (S), and Governance (G)—each scored from 0 to 100. The overall ESG score is the arithmetic mean of the three pillar scores:

Overall ESG Score = (E score + S score + G score) / 3​

Pillar score availability

The pillar scores shown in the profile are computed using the company’s disclosures and a survey questionnaire sent to the company. When there is enough information to score a given pillar, we report its score below. If there is not enough information to score a pillar, it is shown as “_” (underscore).

Your Task
Read the company profile and estimate the company’s overall ESG score (0–100).

Payment

Base payment: $3


Bonus: Up to $5, based on accuracy


You lose $0.25 from your bonus for every point your estimate is away from the true score


Minimum payment: $3


Example
If the true score is 50 and you guess 54, you are 4 points off, so your bonus is $5 − (4 × $0.25) = $4, and your total payment is $7.
